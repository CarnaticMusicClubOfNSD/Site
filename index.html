Our Journey

In 2022, a passionate high school student, Shreya Battula, envisioned a way to share her love for Carnatic music with the younger generation in her community. What began as a small after-school club at Canyon Creek Elementary—with just five curious students—soon blossomed into something far greater. With dedication, heart, and the power of music, the idea grew into a district-wide initiative that has now gained 5+ instructors and chapter heads and introduced over 55 students to this rich tradition of Indian classical music. What started as a humble spark has now become a shining example of how one student’s passion can inspire a community.


This journey wouldn't have been possible without the unwavering support of our Mentors Mr. Elliot Gray (Canyon Creek’s Music and Marimba teacher) and Mr. Srinivas Khedam (NSD's Assistant Director, Family Engagement). From the early days of planning to the club’s flourishing success, their guidance has been instrumental every step of the way.
