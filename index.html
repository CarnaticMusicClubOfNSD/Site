Our Journey and Mission

In 2022, Shreya Battula envisioned a way to share her love for Carnatic music with the younger generation. What began as a small after-school club at Canyon Creek Elementary has grown to inspire 90+ students through a district-wide initiative.


The club is dedicated to making Indian classical music accessible, fostering musical skills alongside confidence and cultural pride. Our goals include:

  • Introduce children to the rich tradition of Carnatic music.
  • Build a youth-led, sustainable leadership model.
  • Encourage discipline, focus, and self-expression.

Family Engagement at NSD

Students follow an 11-week program focusing on:

  • Basic Theory: Understanding the structure of compositions.
  • Vocal Technique: Warmups and pitch accuracy.
  • Sarali Swaras: Core beginner sequences.
  • Showcase: Live performance for families and peers.
